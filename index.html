Creatinine Clearance Estimator Tool

Assumptions

This calculator is based off of Washington Hospital protocols. Some of its assumptions are that
  • Serum creatinine is rounded to 0.8 if the actual value is below
  • Adjusted body weight is used if actual weight is greater or equal to 120% of ideal body weight
  • Ideal body weight is used if actual weight is <120% of ideal body weight
Some special assumptions not part of the protocol are that
  • The Hume equation is used if the patient is less than 60 inches to calculate ideal body weight
